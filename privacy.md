# Privacy Policy

**AbideJourney**
**Effective Date: March 20, 2026**

Abide Journey ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Profile Information**: Your first name and self-described spiritual maturity level, entered during onboarding setup.
- **Journal Entries**: Text reflections you write or dictate, including associated mood selections.
- **Accountability Partners**: Names and email addresses of people you choose to add as accountability partners.
- **Onboarding Responses**: Answers to optional spiritual life questions used to personalize your journey.

### 1.2 Information Generated by the App

- **Journey Progress**: Which days you have completed, action step completion, and check-in ratings.
- **Prayer Sessions**: Start time and duration of prayer timer sessions.
- **Notification Preferences**: Your chosen morning and evening reminder times.
- **Subscription Status**: Whether you have an active Premium subscription (managed entirely by Apple).

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect your email address, phone number, or physical address.
- We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data.
- We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs.
- We do not track your activity across other apps or websites.

## 2. How Your Data Is Stored

All of your personal data is stored **locally on your device** using Apple's on-device database technology (SwiftData). Your data is not uploaded to our servers because we do not operate any servers.

The only exceptions where data may leave your device are described below in Section 3.

## 3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

### 3.1 Apple iCloud (Watch App Sync)

If you use the Abide Journey Apple Watch app, your profile, journey progress, and prayer sessions are synced to your **private iCloud container** using Apple's CloudKit technology. This data is encrypted and governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We cannot access your iCloud data.

### 3.2 Apple Health (HealthKit)

With your explicit permission, the App writes prayer session durations to Apple Health as Mindfulness minutes and reads your existing mindfulness data to display weekly summaries. This data is governed by Apple's HealthKit policies and never leaves your device except through Apple Health's own sharing mechanisms. You can revoke this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.

We do not use HealthKit data for advertising or marketing purposes, and we do not sell or share HealthKit data with third parties.

### 3.3 Apple Speech Recognition

When you use the voice journal feature, your speech is processed by Apple's on-device Speech framework to convert it to text. Audio is not recorded, stored, or transmitted by the App. You can revoke microphone and speech recognition permissions at any time in your device Settings.

### 3.4 Apple App Store (In-App Purchases)

Premium subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card number, or Apple ID. Subscriptions are governed by [Apple's Terms and Conditions](https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/).

### 3.5 Share Sheet

When you share progress updates, encouragement messages, or accountability partner invitations, content is passed to Apple's system share sheet. The App does not control or track what you do with the share sheet.

### 3.6 No Other Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to any third party. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, social media SDKs, or external APIs.

## 4. Notifications

With your permission, the App sends local notifications to remind you of your daily devotional and evening check-in. Notification content may include your current day number and a brief scripture snippet. You can disable notifications at any time in the App's Settings or in your device's notification settings.

## 5. Data Export

Premium subscribers can export their journal entries as a PDF file. This file is generated locally on your device and is not uploaded anywhere unless you choose to share it.

## 6. Data Deletion

You can delete all of your data at any time by going to **Settings → Delete All Data & Reset**. This permanently removes your profile, all journeys, journal entries, prayer sessions, check-ins, quiz responses, and accountability partners from your device. This action cannot be undone.

If you use the Apple Watch app, you should also delete the Watch app to remove synced data from iCloud.

Alternatively, you can uninstall the App, which removes all locally stored data.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Abide Journey is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can delete it.

## 8. Security

Your data is protected by Apple's built-in device encryption and, where applicable, iCloud encryption. Since we do not operate servers or collect your data remotely, there is no centralized database that could be breached.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 10. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** all data the App stores about you (visible within the App itself).
- **Delete** all your data at any time (Settings → Delete All Data & Reset).
- **Revoke** permissions for Health, microphone, speech recognition, and notifications at any time through your device Settings.
- **Cancel** your Premium subscription at any time through Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
